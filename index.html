About Blackpink

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Lisa, and Rose. The group debuted on August 8, 2016, with their single Square One, which spawned "Whistle," their first number-one song in South Korea

Why people love Blackpink

The most significant part of BLACKPINK's popularity lies in the group's undeniable stage presence and their songs and bilingual lyrics, which talk about independence and vulnerability. BLACKPINK is also known for their video styling or "concepts" for each song.
